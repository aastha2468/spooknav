About spookynav


This project was created for the 2020 Technica hackathon.

spookynav is a one-stop shop of safety data from datatsets in the Baltimore area. Parents can see the safety rating of different areas based on sex offender data, gun offender data, and number of CCTV cameras. With this inclusive data visualization tool, parents can be assured that their children can enjoy Halloween safely.

Created by Mira Tellegen, Aastha Senjalia, Ash Freels, and Andrea Tongsak
